Description

Toasted bread with, or without a spread coating the top. Very versatile, can be combined with many different dishes, or served by itself as a breakfast, snack or a really easy lunch/dinner.

Ingredients

  • Bread (Not already toasted)
  • Any Extras

Method

  1. Toast the bread Put bread in a toaster and start toasting it, until you are satisfied with how toasted the bread is.
  2. Enjoy! Remove the toast and add anything else you'd like to to the toast. This could be butter, peanut butter or even jam.
